Setting up

In the settings of your ghost admin, add a custom integration. Here I added credentials to a file, containing the host name (without https://www.), a space, and the api-key (which consists of two parts, connected with a colon). 

ghostInstance := GtGhostInstance new.
ghostInstance readCredentialsFrom: (FileLocator home /'.ghost/.integrations/ghost') pathString.
ghostInstance testConnection
